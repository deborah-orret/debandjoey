Getting There & Parking

The ceremony and cocktail hour will take place inside the Mathias Botanical Gardens on UCLA's campus. The reception will be held right outside at the La Kretz Garden Pavilion. The address to map to is 707 Tiverton Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90095.

You will need to enter through the northern entrance of the gardens. Navigate to the corner of Tiverton Drive & Charles E Young - then follow the path down.

Parking is available at UCLA Parking Structure 27 or UCLA Parking Structure 2 (pay-by-plate). Street parking is available along Hilgard Ave.

We'll have signs from the parking structures to guide you to the entrance!
